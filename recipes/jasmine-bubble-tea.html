Jasmine Milk Bubble Tea

This jasmine milk tea is based on green tea & any milk of your choice. It's an easy refreshment and a cheap DIY alternative to store-bought bubble tea. Save money and learn something new by making this boba yourself!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Boba

Steps

  1. Make jasmine tea in a mug. Set it aside to let it cool.
  2. In a small pan, cook the tapioca pearls. Then remove from heat & cover them with sugar.
  3. Add boba pearls & ice cubes to a glass.
  4. Pour milk over them.
  5. Then add cooled down jasmine tea. Mix well & enjoy!
